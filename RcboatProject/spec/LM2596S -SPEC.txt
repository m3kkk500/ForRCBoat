LM2596S -SPEC
Performance and characteristics module:
● Voltmeter display and self-calibration. Advanced microprocessor voltage is used, the voltmeter error is ± 0.05V, and the range is 0~40V (to ensure the accuracy of the voltmeter, please ensure that the input voltage is higher than 4.5V).
● Touch the input or output voltage measurement switch key and the indicator light will display which voltage is being measured, and the setting will be reset even if the power is cut off and the machine starts again.
● The voltmeter can be turned off, easily realized by touching the left button when not needed.
● Terminal Blocks can be easily used without welding machine, and welding wire connection points.
● Input voltage 4.0 ~ 40V (voltage must be 1.5V higher than the output voltage)
● Adjustable output voltage range: 1.25V ~ 37V continuously (voltage must be 1.5V higher than the output voltage)
● Output current 3A, it is recommended to use within 2.0A. Heat dissipation shall be strengthened with large current.
● Output power 20W. If it exceeds 15W, please enhance heat dissipation.
● Conversion efficiency, average 88% (efficiency is related to input and output voltage, current and differential voltage)
● Reverse connection protection, reverse connection does not burn.
● Overheat and short circuit protection function
● Size: 6.1*3.4*12cm (L * W * H)
● Weight: 22g
